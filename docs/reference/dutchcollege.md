# Dutch college data

Longitudinal relational measurements and nodal characteristics of Dutch
college students, described in van de Bunt, van Duijn, and Snijders
(1999). The time interval between the first four measurements was three
weeks, whereas the interval between the last three was six weeks.

## Format

A list consisting of a socioarray `Y` and a matrix `X` of static nodal
attributes. The relational measurements range from -1 to 4, indicating
the following:

- -1 a troubled or negative relationship

- 0 don't know

- 1 neutral relationship

- 2 friendly

- 3 friendship

- 4 best friends

## Source

Originally available at `http://moreno.ss.uci.edu/data.html#vdb` (site
no longer accessible).
